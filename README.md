# DIGITAL-LITERACY
Name:- Divyani Mishra
Reg Number:- 25BCE11379
Digital literacy is an individual's ability to find, evaluate, and communicate information using digital devices or digital media platforms. Digital literacy combines technical and cognitive abilities; it includes using information and communication technologies to create, evaluate, and share information,[1] and critically examining their social and political impacts.[2]

Digital literacy initially focused on digital skills and stand-alone computers, but the advent of the internet and social media use has shifted some of its focus to mobile devices.[3]
